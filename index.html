    Banglore

    I'm Krsna, an amateur photographer based in Bangalore. My lens is my storyteller, specializing in monochrome photography that captures the heartbeat of this vibrant city. Through shades of black and white, I freeze the moments that define Bangalore's bustling streets, revealing hidden narratives and emotions. Each photograph is a visual journey, an exploration of simplicity and the extraordinary within the ordinary. My work goes beyond documentation; it's a personal quest to distill the essence of Bangalore, showcasing its diverse facets and the timeless beauty found in the interplay of light and shadow. Join me as I navigate the visual poetry of this city.
